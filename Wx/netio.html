Weather Info for NetIO

The Weather plug-in supplies built-in "custom" procedures (netio, netioaction and get_update) to support NetIO.

Triggering a Weather Fetch via NetIO

A weather fetch can be triggered in the NetIO application by the netioaction command: For the image to be displayed, you must manually download image files from the appropriate weather web site and pre-load them into the NetIO application's data space. Since there are dozens of possible weather icons, getting icons may not be worth the effort.
